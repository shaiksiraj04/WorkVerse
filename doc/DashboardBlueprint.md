# Purpose

The Employee Dashboard is the central workspace of every Backend Engineering Intern.

It should feel like a modern employee portal rather than an online learning dashboard.

The dashboard must immediately answer three questions:

• What is my current work?
• How am I performing?
• What should I do next?

---

# Dashboard Goals

The dashboard should:

- Motivate users to continue their internship.
- Show clear progress.
- Display today's work.
- Reduce confusion.
- Make the learner feel like an employee.
