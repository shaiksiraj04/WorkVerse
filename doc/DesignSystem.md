# 1. Brand Identity

## Brand Name

WorkVerse

---

## Tagline

**Experience the job before you get the job.**

---

## Mission

Help students and fresh graduates experience real IT and Cybersecurity industry work before entering their first job through practical, role-based learning.

---

## Vision

To become the world's most trusted career simulation platform that bridges the gap between education and industry.

---

## Brand Personality

WorkVerse should feel:

- Professional
- Modern
- Innovative
- Friendly
- Motivating
- Trustworthy
- Gamified
- Futuristic

---

## Core Values

- Learn by Doing
- Practical Experience
- Continuous Improvement
- Career Readiness
- Accessibility
- Innovation

---

## Target Audience

- Final Year Students
- Fresh Graduates
- Career Switchers

Age Group:

16–35 Years

Primary Country:

India

Primary Language:

English

---

## Brand Promise

Every learner should leave WorkVerse with more confidence, practical experience, and a better understanding of how real software companies operate.
