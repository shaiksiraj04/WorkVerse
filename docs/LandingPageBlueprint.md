# Purpose

This document defines the structure, content, goals, and user experience of the WorkVerse landing page.

The landing page should immediately communicate the value of WorkVerse and convince visitors to begin their internship journey.

The first impression should feel premium, modern, and trustworthy.

---

# Landing Page Goals

The landing page should answer these questions within the first 10 seconds:

- What is WorkVerse?
- Who is it for?
- Why is it different?
- Why should I join?
- How do I get started?
