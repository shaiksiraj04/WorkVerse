# 💼 Internship Program Document

# Backend Engineering Internship

**Version:** 1.0

**Company:** WorkVerse Technologies

**Status:** MVP Program

**Prepared By:** WorkVerse Product Team

---

# 1. Overview

The Backend Engineering Internship is the first internship program offered by WorkVerse Technologies.

It provides aspiring backend engineers with a realistic software engineering experience through simulated workplace assignments, engineering sprints, AI mentorship, and performance reviews.

The internship is designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and real industry work.

---

# 2. Objective

The objective of this internship is to prepare learners for their first backend engineering job by allowing them to experience realistic engineering workflows before entering the industry.

Students should finish the internship feeling confident enough to understand how backend development teams operate.

---

# 3. Target Audience

This internship is designed for:

- Final-Year Students
- Fresh Graduates
- Self-Learners
- Career Switchers

Recommended Age:

16–35 Years

Language:

English

---

# 4. Internship Duration

Total Duration:

8 Weeks

Daily Commitment:

20–30 Minutes

Estimated Total Learning Time:

25–30 Hours

---

# 5. Learning Philosophy

The internship follows a practical learning approach.

Instead of watching long lectures, learners complete realistic work assignments that simulate real backend engineering tasks.

The emphasis is on:

- Understanding
- Practical implementation
- Continuous improvement
- Professional thinking
